A: Yes! Update whenever you want. Weekly is recommended for accuracy.

A: No problem! Update when you remember. The data persists.

A: No! Manual Edit works without any programming knowledge.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! Everything is stored locally in browser or in your GitHub repo.
